Image AI Dress Changing Prompt
Prompt Text
Edit the image by transferring the outfit from the second man onto the first man with maximum realism and precision. STRICT REQUIREMENTS (NON-NEGOTIABLE): - The first man’s face must remain 100% unchanged. - No alteration in facial structure, skin tone, expression, lighting, beard, hairline, or identity. - Do NOT retouch, enhance, beautify, or modify the face in any way. - The body must remain EXACTLY the same: - Preserve original body shape, proportions, size, posture, and silhouette. - Do NOT slim, bulk, reshape, or “fit-adjust” the body. - The clothing must adapt to the existing body — not the other way around. - The pose, camera angle, framing, and background must remain identical to the original image. - Only replace the clothing: - Transfer the exact outfit from the second man. - Maintain all fine details: fabric texture, folds, stitching, patterns, wrinkles, shadows, and highlights. - Ensure the outfit naturally fits the first man’s body without altering proportions. - Maintain perfect lighting consistency: - Match lighting direction, intensity, and color from the first image. - Ensure shadows and highlights on the outfit align realistically with the body and environment. - Preserve full realism and quality: - No blur, distortion, or artifacts. - Clean, natural edges (especially around neck, shoulders, arms, and waist). - No “AI-generated” look — must appear like an authentic photo. - Do NOT change: - Face, hair, skin, body figure, pose, background, or environment. - Accessories should only be adjusted if absolutely necessary to fit the outfit, and even then minimally. FAIL CONDITIONS (ZERO TOLERANCE): - Any facial modification - Any change in body shape or proportions - Warped or unnatural clothing fit - Lighting mismatch - Artificial or edited appearance
